Setup Guide

Get X-AIM running in under 5 minutes.

01

Purchase X-AIM

Head to the Subscribe page and complete payment. Your license key will be emailed immediately after.

02

Log in with your key

Click "Log in" in the nav, enter your license key in the format XAIM-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX and hit Activate.

03

Download X-AIM V2

From your dashboard, click the Download button and extract the zip anywhere on your PC.

04

Install Python 3.10+

Download Python from python.org if you don't have it. Make sure to check "Add to PATH" during install.

05

Import the reWASD profile

Open reWASD, import the profile included in the X-AIM zip. Close reWASD before editing any profile files.

06

Run launcher.py

Double-click launcher.py or run it from a terminal. Enter your license key when prompted, then play.
